Student tortilla

Description

Quick 15min recipie for students

Ingridients

  • Tortilla
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Ham
  • Peper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut tomatos and mozzarella in cubes
  2. Heat tortilla for 15 sec
  3. Put slices of ham on tortilla
  4. Add sliced mozzarella and tomatoes on ham
  5. Fold the tortilla and put it into grill
