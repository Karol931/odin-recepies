Makaron z pomidorem i mozarella

Description

Quick 15min recipie for students

Ingridients

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Bazil
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut tomatos and mozzarella in cubes
  2. Boil Pasta
  3. Poor olive oil over tomatoes and mozzarella
  4. Add bazil and salt to sauce
  5. Mix sauce together and put it on pasta
